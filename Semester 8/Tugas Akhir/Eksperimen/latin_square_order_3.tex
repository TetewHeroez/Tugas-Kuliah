\subsection*{Latin Square Order 3}

Terdapat 12 Latin square berbeda dengan order 3 dan simbol $\{1, 2, 3\}$:

$L_{1} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 2 & 3 & 1 \\ 3 & 1 & 2 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \begin{matrix} \tau_{1} = (1)(2 3) \\ \tau_{2} = (1 2)(3) \\ \tau_{3} = (1 3)(2) \end{matrix}$

$L_{5} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 & 3 \\ 1 & 3 & 2 \\ 3 & 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \begin{matrix} \tau_{1} = (1 2)(3) \\ \tau_{2} = (1)(2 3) \\ \tau_{3} = (1 3)(2) \end{matrix}$

$L_{6} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 & 3 \\ 3 & 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 & 2 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \begin{matrix} \tau_{1} = (1 2 3) \\ \tau_{2} = (1)(2)(3) \\ \tau_{3} = (1 3 2) \end{matrix}$
